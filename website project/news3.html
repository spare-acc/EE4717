Roger Federer - A Great among the Greats.

Roger Federer is a Swiss professional tennis player who is currently ranked No. 3 in men's singles tennis, and he held the highest ranking of No. 1 for a record 310 weeks.

He has won 20 GRAND SLAM singles titles - the most in history by a male player.

Federer has won a record eight Wimbledon titles, six Australian Open titles, five US Open titles (all consecutive, a record), and one French Open title.

He is one of eight men to have achieved a Career Grand Slam.

TFederer's all-court game and versatile style of play involve exceptional footwork and shot-making.[4] Effective both as a baseliner and a volleyer, his apparent effortlessness and efficient movement on the court have made Federer highly popular among tennis fans.
