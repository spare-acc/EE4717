Singapore's Yeo Jia Min goes through to the quarterfinals.

Singapore woman badminton player Yeo Jia Min beat world number one Akane Yamaguchi 21-14, 21-18 at the BWF World Championships on Tuesday night, Aug 20.

Her straight sets win took 39 minutes.

She was leading 11-5 just five minutes into the first game.

Yeo commented after her victory:"Today, I did my best to focus on very point from the start, to be patient but ready for chance to attack."
