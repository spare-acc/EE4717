England facing a tricky encounter in Qualifiers.

England, with its team of young guns are raring to go in their bid toqualigy for EURO 2020.

However standing in their path are two tricky games against Wales and Portugal who are being lead by Gareth Bale and Cristiano Ronaldo respectively.

Odds are slightly in England's favor but the boss himself says there are no rooms for complacency.

More than half of England's squad is under 25 years of age.

The people are looking forward to seeing the young guns upstaged their counter part.
